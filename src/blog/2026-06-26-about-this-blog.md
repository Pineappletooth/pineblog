I wanted to make a personal webpage since a long time ago, but never had the motivation to do it, until recently when i wanted to write some opinions that where long in my head i didn't had a place to do it.

This will contain mainly tech opinions, current experiences, my experiments with new technologies, etc.

## Design

My inspirations were the neocities.org vibes and the Oxocarbon colorscheme (which i use a variation of in my Neovim setup) although colors might remember more to Tokyonight, at first i imagined the site more professional and minimalist, but after some exploring i thought in something that better suits me.

After that I don't have much design skills I so just iterated colors and some ideas until i found something i was satisfied.

## On AI usage

I am on favor of responsible AI use, i found it practical for finding fitting colors (thats how i adapt my colorschemes) for asking some questions or debugging, but I don't think creative work like drawing, UI design or writing should be done by a machine, after all creativity is what differentiate humans from other species.
